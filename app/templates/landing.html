Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to find answers to common queries about ClickHR's services, features, security measures, customization options, and support offerings.

  • What is ClickHR and how does it work?

    ClickHR is a web-based platform designed to streamline recruitment processes for staffing companies. It works by integrating with external data sources, such as Ionos webmail and Indeed, to automate candidate management, job postings, and reporting.

  • What features does ClickHR offer for recruiters?

    ClickHR offers recruiters a range of features including candidate management tools, job posting and distribution capabilities, advanced reporting and analytics, role-based access control, and integrated email notifications.

  • How does ClickHR ensure data security and privacy?

    ClickHR implements robust security measures such as password encryption, role-based access control, and secure data handling practices to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of user data.

  • Can ClickHR be customized to suit specific organizational needs?

    Yes, ClickHR's user interface is customizable to meet the specific needs of different users within an organization. Additionally, role-based access control allows administrators to tailor permissions and functionalities according to user roles.

  • What support options are available for ClickHR users?

    ClickHR provides comprehensive support options including online documentation, tutorials, and user guides. Additionally, users can access technical support via email or phone for assistance with any issues or questions they may have.
