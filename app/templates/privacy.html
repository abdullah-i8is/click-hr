Privacy Policy

Welcome to ClickHR

This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect and process your personal data when you use our website and services. By accessing or using ClickHR, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information when you interact with our website and services. This includes:

  • Information you provide directly: When you fill out forms, register for our services, or communicate with us via email, phone, or other means.
  • Information collected automatically: Such as your location, traffic data, weblogs, and communication data when you visit our website.

2. Application of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to any personal data collected from you during your use of ClickHR's website and services. It also applies to personal information collected when you contact us via email or through our Contact Form.

3. Exclusions

This Privacy Policy does not apply to information provided by third parties or publicly available with no restrictions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

4. Security Measures

We take the security and confidentiality of your personal information seriously. We have implemented physical, electronic, and administrative measures to prevent unauthorized access to your data. Only authorized personnel and our trusted Data Processors have access to your personal information, and they handle it confidentially.

While we employ reasonable security measures, please note that no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Information Usage

We may use the personal information you provide for various purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Providing you with relevant information about our products and services
  • Analyzing how you use our website and services to improve user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. This includes maintaining accurate records, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. Once your data is no longer needed, we securely dispose of it in accordance with our data retention policies and applicable laws.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at clickhr@click-hr.com.
